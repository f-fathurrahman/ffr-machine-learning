Add noise to our model explicitly.
\begin{equation*}
t_{n} = \mathbf{w}^{\mathsf{T}}\mathbf{x}_{n} + \epsilon_{n}
\end{equation*}
$\epsilon_{n}$ is a continuous random variable.

\begin{equation}
p(\epsilon_{1},\epsilon_{2},\ldots,\epsilon_{N}) = \prod_{n=1}^{N} p(\epsilon_{n})
\end{equation}
